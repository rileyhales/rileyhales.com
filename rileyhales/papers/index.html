ACAE Infrastructure Scholarship Competition

Paper Submitted 18 January 2019


  • Predicting Streamflow in Cuba for Water Resource Management

The Association of Cuban American Engineers holds an annual paper writing and presentation contest for a 6 thousand dollar scholarship. The subject is Cuban infrastructure. My team of three wrote an approximately 4 thousand word paper on how to use the global modeling and forecasting capabilities developed in the Hydroinformatics lab where i was working at the time. I was the principal author responsible for the majority of the writing and technical content. We invited them to adopt the technology discussed and join international communities as a way to improve their country, save lives and money, and promote greater international cooperation
